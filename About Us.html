About the Project

The Online Real-time Survey and Monitoring of Water Bodies in Delhi project aims to provide a technological solution for the rejuvenation and sustainable monitoring of water bodies in Delhi. Our project uses cutting-edge tools and real-time data collection methods to monitor the water levels, quality, and overall condition of various water bodies. This solution will help authorities and environmentalists make data-driven decisions for the maintenance and restoration of these critical resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to utilize modern technology for the rejuvenation and sustainable management of water bodies in Delhi. By collecting and analyzing real-time data, we aim to provide accurate information about water quality and quantity to the relevant authorities. Our project will ensure that the water bodies of Delhi are maintained and preserved for future generations.
